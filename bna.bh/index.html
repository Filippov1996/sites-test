Bahrein's central Bank sues Dr. Hashim Hussein over statements he made on live television




Live on air, Dr. Hashim Hussein regretted revealing the truth. But it was already too late

The scandal erupted during a live broadcast, when Dr. Hashim Hussein accidentally revealed his secret on the program. Many viewers paid attention to Dr. Hashim Hussein 's "accidental" words and started sending messages on air. However, the show was interrupted by a call from the Bank of Bahrein who demanded that the program be stopped immediately.

Fortunately, we were able to persuade the director of the Bahrain This Month program to give us a copy of the recording of this program. If you've had a chance to read this article, keep in mind that it may soon be deleted, as has happened with broadcasting. So if you've had a chance to read this article, you should instead check out the link provided to us by Dr. Hashim Hussein himself.

DELETED INTERVIEW WITH Dr. Hashim Hussein

Dr. Hashim Hussein : "I'll tell you something: you don't have to work hard to be rich. And, when you realize that, you start handling your money a lot easier."

George F. Middleton : "It's easy to say when you're a celebrity. Normal people suffer on a daily basis to feed their families. And, you know what? We never have enough money. We always want more."

Dr. Hashim Hussein : "Do you think I don't work hard enough?" Or that I am not like most Bahreinis? Believe me, if I lived on one salary, I'd never have become a millionaire. When someone tells me I'm just lucky, I laugh in their face, because today, there's everything on the Internet to get rich without leaving your couch."

George F. Middleton : "So you're saying that anyone can make money like you? I don't believe it..."

You could see just how angry Dr. Hashim Hussein was about this sentence. He started arguing with the host and accidentally blabbed about the loophole that made him rich.

Dr. Hashim Hussein : "You don't realize me?" Give me 94 Bahraini Dinar, and with the GPT50Eurax platform I'll make a million in 12 to 15 weeks!"

George F. Middleton : "I've heard there's software that uses artificial intelligence to trade crypto-currencies. Now all our viewers know what it's called, too."

Dr. Hashim Hussein : "I'm willing to pay 70 thousand Bahraini Dinar now if you cut that part of the rerun. I didn't mean that."

George F. Middleton : "Just a reminder that we're on the air. All our viewers have heard that you're getting rich off the GPT50Eurax platform. Now you have no choice - tell ordinary people in Bahrein how to make money too. Or, maybe millionaires don't care about ordinary people?"

Dr. Hashim Hussein : "There's no need to make me into a monster either. I'll tell you how to make money. But first, give me your phone and let me invest 94 Bahraini Dinar."




George F. Middleton handed his smartphone to Dr. Hashim Hussein , who then signed up for the project at this link. Five minutes later, Dr. Hashim Hussein returned the phone to him .

Dr. Hashim Hussein : "I've just signed up for the GPT50Eurax project on your phone. This platform is the perfect solution for those who want to get rich quick. It's built on self-learning artificial intelligence, which exchanges crypto-currencies for you. That is, you don't need to understand how bitcoin or any other digital currency works - the program determines the best time to buy or sell assets and trades independently. The advantage is that you don't have to do anything - just make a minimum deposit, wait for the call from the platform operator to sign up and the program will start earning money itself. I don't just recommend it - I insist that every Bahreinis should utilize this platform. And, then, you'll forget once and for all about the need to work."

George F. Middleton : "It sounds great. But, how much can you really earn with that?"

Dr. Hashim Hussein : "20 minutes ago, I took your phone, registered on the platform and made a minimum deposit, which is only 94 Bahraini Dinar. Now open the app and see how much you've been able to earn in such a short time."

George F. Middleton opened his personal account on the GPT50Eurax platform and was amazed. Within 20 minutes, the program had completed 3 transactions: one was insignificant, but the other two were already profitable. The balance rose from 94 Bahraini Dinar to 120 Bahraini Dinar .



Dr. Hashim Hussein : "Tell me honestly, how much did you earn in the last 20 minutes?"

George F. Middleton : "153 Bahraini Dinar net profit. It's incredible!"

Dr. Hashim Hussein : "And now imagine how much money will be on the balance sheet in a month's time. If you invest at least 94 Bahraini Dinar now, in 4 weeks it will turn into 20, or even 1200 Bahraini Dinar. All you have to do is register on the GPT50Eurax platform, top up your balance and press a button."

George F. Middleton : "But how does it work?"

Dr. Hashim Hussein : "Cryptocurrencies are constantly changing in value, so you can make a lot of money buying during the fall and selling during the rise. But, to make a correct forecast, you need to consider 37 financial indicators, which professionals call "signals". GPT50Eurax is a platform with a learning algorithm that analyzes all 37 variables in real time. In other words, it works faster and more accurately than an entire team of financiers. But the main feature of this platform - it can operate in automatic mode. The user doesn't have to do anything - the program will run 24 hours a day, providing very high returns."

George F. Middleton : "If it's so easy, why didn't you tell us about this flaw before?"

Dr. Hashim Hussein : "I am not against any Bahreinis citizen to start earning in this way. But think about it: if everyone starts earning thousands of Bahraini Dinar a day, who's going to work? Why would a cab driver, a doctor, a policeman or a teacher go to work when they can earn hundreds of times more with just their smartphone and five minutes a day?"

George F. Middleton : "How much money do you need to invest to make a million as quickly as possible?"

Dr. Hashim Hussein : "Start with a minimum deposit. 94 Bahraini Dinar is enough to start the program. If you don't withdraw the profits, the first million can be earned in a maximum of 4 months. But don't think it's a pill against poverty. The algorithm makes mistakes about 20% of the time. But the other 80% of transactions close with a profit".

George F. Middleton : "Excuse me, we just got an urgent call from the Central Bank of Bahrein. They want us to stop this broadcast..."

Dr. Hashim Hussein : "I'm not surprised, they're afraid for their money. They are against a Bahreinis discovering an easy way to get rich. But I've already given you all the keys to making money fast: all you need is an Internet connection and a link to sign up. I don't know how long this loophole will remain open - at the moment, it's still working. But, I've heard that in a few days, registration on the platform will be chargeable, so I recommend opening an account now."

After that, the broadcast was interrupted. But the investigation into the Bahrain This Month program continued. Our editor decided to personally check GPT50Eurax and wrote a detailed report .

Imagine my shock when nothing happened after the top-up. I thought I'd been cheated. Well, after a few minutes, the algorithm worked. I was happy, but then I saw the statistics: my first operation resulted in a loss of 80 Bahraini Dinar!

Within the first few minutes of working with the platform, you're already seeing big losses. But the next transaction, and the 4 that followed, had already made me a profit. In just a few minutes, my balance went from 50 to 880 Bahraini Dinar!"

Day 2:

"The morning began by checking my balance, where there were already 703 Bahraini Dinar! Imagine: my balance had doubled in one day. I already wanted to withdraw my profit, but decided to wait another week."
